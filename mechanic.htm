League of Legend's Turret Plates

Debuted in Season 9, turret plates introduced a wide range of playstyles for players while also extending the duration of matches. Obtaining turret plates posed a high-risk, high-reward scenario, accelerating itemization and potentially allowing for a snowball effect or aiding weaker teams in defending during the early game. Riot Games has invested considerable effort in fine-tuning this mechanic through numerous patches, and it appears to have achieved a satisfactory balance in the current state of the game.

Dota 2's Stacking neutral creep camp

Stacking in 'Dota 2' involves luring neutral creeps away from their spawn area at specific timings, typically around the 53rd-second mark on the in-game clock (e.g., 0:53, 1:53). This creates additional camps of creeps that players can farm to enhance their economy. This tactic encourages players to stack as many camps as possible to boost their resources and strategically reset creep waves for safer positioning, reducing vulnerability to enemy ganks.

Genshin Impact's Elemental Gauge Theory

The core principles of the Elemental Gauge Theory encompass elemental attack auras and the gauge cost associated with elemental reactions. The Gauge Unit, also referred to as the Elemental Gauge, represents the measure of an element's potency tied to a character's ability. It can be likened to the intensity or quantity of the applied element. In the case of an aura generated by an elemental attack, there exists an "Aura Tax" that deducts 20% of the gauge linked to the created aura. Grasping this mechanic provides players with a substantial advantage when it comes to character development, team composition, and strategic planning.
